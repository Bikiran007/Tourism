About Kashmir

Kashmir, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth," is a region of extraordinary beauty nestled in the northern part of the Indian subcontinent. With its snow-capped mountains, pristine lakes, lush green valleys, and rich cultural heritage, Kashmir offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

From the famous dal lake in Srinagar to the majestic Gulmarg meadows, from the ancient temples to the vibrant local markets, Kashmir is a destination that captivates the heart and soul of every visitor.

Whether you're seeking adventure in the Himalayas, tranquility by the lakeside, or a cultural immersion in the local traditions, Kashmir has something special for everyone.

Top Attractions

Dal Lake

The jewel of Srinagar, famous for its houseboats and shikara rides amidst beautiful Mughal gardens.

Gulmarg

Known as the "Meadow of Flowers," this popular ski destination offers breathtaking views and adventure sports.

Pahalgam

A serene valley with lush meadows, pine forests, and the Lidder River, perfect for trekking and nature walks.

Sonamarg

The "Meadow of Gold" serves as the gateway to the Amarnath Yatra and offers stunning glacier views.
