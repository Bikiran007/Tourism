Beautiful Tourist Spots in Kashmir

Dal Lake, Srinagar

Famous for its houseboats and shikaras, Dal Lake is the heart of Srinagar's beauty.

Gulmarg

Known for skiing, lush meadows, and the Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world.

Pahalgam

A peaceful town with pine forests, rivers, and the starting point for the Amarnath Yatra.

Sonamarg

Called the 'Meadow of Gold', Sonamarg offers majestic views and access to glaciers and trekking routes.

Betaab Valley

Named after a Bollywood movie, this valley is a scenic spot with greenery and crystal-clear streams.
