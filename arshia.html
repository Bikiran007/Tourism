Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kashmir safe for tourists?

Yes, Kashmir is generally safe for tourists. Check local travel advisories and follow guidelines.

When is the best time to visit Kashmir?

March to October for lush greenery, and December to February for snow sports.

What should I pack?

Warm clothes (especially in winter), comfortable shoes, sunscreen, and a camera!

Travel Tips for Kashmir

  • Carry a valid ID and copies of your travel documents.
  • Dress in layers—weather can change quickly in the mountains.
  • Try local dishes like Rogan Josh, Kahwa tea, and Kashmiri Pulao.
  • Respect local customs and avoid restricted areas.
