Welcome to the Paradise on Earth – Kashmir

Nestled in the lap of the majestic Himalayas, Kashmir is a land of breathtaking beauty, serene lakes, snow-capped mountains, and vibrant culture. Whether you're cruising on a shikara in Dal Lake, skiing in Gulmarg, or exploring the gardens of Srinagar, Kashmir offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

Gulmarg

Famous for its snow sports and the highest cable car in Asia, Gulmarg is a winter wonderland and summer retreat all in one.

Dal Lake

Take a peaceful shikara ride or stay in a charming houseboat — Dal Lake is the heart of Srinagar and a symbol of Kashmiri beauty.

Pahalgam

A scenic town surrounded by pine forests and rivers, ideal for nature walks, river rafting, and pony rides to the meadows.
